● Space Flight Simulator

A 2-hour guided series building a 3D space flight simulator from scratch, introducing key concepts in physics, 3D graphics, and game development.

LESSON 01
The Canvas and Coordinates
Drawing pixels, the 2D coordinate system, the game loop, and delta time
LESSON 02
Vectors — The Language of Movement
The Vec3 class, vector operations, and using WASD to move in 2D
LESSON 03
3D Perspective Projection
World space, camera space, the projection formula, and depth sorting
LESSON 04
Camera Orientation — Pitch and Yaw
Euler angles, forward/right/up vectors, transforming world→camera space
LESSON 05
Thrust and Physics
Acceleration, Newton's laws, zero-gravity drift, WASD rocket boosters
LESSON 06
Complete Flight Simulator
Everything combined — full HUD, planets, zero-G flight. Fly!
